Company

Cengage Learning

Challenge

Cengage was in need of a low cost, fast turnaround homework solution that could be included with any number of titles across disciplines. At the same time, the content of the titles would vary and the product had to adapt.

Solution

We created a modular design where product teams could pull from any of our libraries of functionality based upon the content they had readily available:

  • Graded quizzes
  • Practice assessments
  • Case studies
  • Vocabulary based games (hangman, flash cards, concentration, spelling bee)
  • Question-based games (Jeopardy knock off, Tic-tac-toe)
  • Audio dictionary
  • Image labeling exercises
  • Media library

Automated content conversion from the internal formats used by the customer to the final XML format used by the StudyWARE shell. All content was kept external to the programming to facilitate maintenance and updates.

Target turnaround from project kickoff to final delivery was 6 weeks.

We delivered a fully brandable shell, so that titles would match look and feel of the associated title.

We delivered 25-50 titles per year over 4 major revisions of the shell, on-time and on-budget.

Company

Jones and Bartlett Learning

Challenge

To develop a custom training continuing education course for Athletic Trainers on the subject of Preventing Sudden Death in Sport and Physical Activities.

Solution

We worked with the Korey Stringer Institute (internationally known, named for Vikings player who died from heat illness) to develop all content.

Interactives handled all aspects of the program from instructional design, script writing, working with video production, programming, etc.

Combination of instructional content as well as two ways to practice dealing with life threatening emergencies:

  • Case studies where users are guided through the situation and given feedback directing them to the proper procedures of dealing with an event.
  • Time video-based branching exercises that demand the user address the situation at hand in the correct sequence and with timeliness.
