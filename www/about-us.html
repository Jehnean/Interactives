About Interactives

Interactive Solutions was founded in 1992 in the very early days of eLearning.

Mark Welcome founded Interactives in 1992 after several years of working with IBM’s academic information systems department. He started the company to support the higher education market, and over the years as the market matured, he added more services and a team of people to support new business needs. The company expanded to include people with skills in project management, instructional development, computer programming, video creation, video editing and graphic design.

As a leading developer of eLearning, our goal at Interactives is to make our customers’ lives easier. Clients rely on our:

Experience

We have been in the eLearning business since 1992 (pre-Internet!)

Longterm Relationships

Many of our client relationships span multiple companies and multiple decades.

Reliability

We ship on time and on budget. We guarantee our work and support our products long after they have been delivered.

Collaborative Approach

We build true partnerships with our customers: understanding your business and your eLearning goals and working with you to achieve them.
